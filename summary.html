Summarization of Our Stream

On February 4, 2025, our group hosted a 2.5-hour livestream on Twitch featuring popular Roblox games suggested by friends. The stream included engaging chat interactions, participation from classmates, and a fun group call with four members. The positive presence of friends added to the lively and entertaining atmosphere. We chose to stream gameplay sessions because it's a fun, easy-to-produce format that allows for spontaneous audience interaction, and we share a genuine love for Roblox and gaming.
